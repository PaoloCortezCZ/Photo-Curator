PHOTO CURATOR v6.0 — Offline, for Apple Silicon Macs (M1/M2/M3/M4)

TO START:
1. Keep this whole "PhotoCurator" folder together (don't move files out).
2. Right-click (or Control-click) "Start Photo Curator.command"
   -> choose "Open" -> click "Open" in the dialog.
   (Right-click → Open is needed only the FIRST time. After that you can
   just double-click it.)
3. A small Terminal window opens showing progress, and your browser
   opens automatically at http://127.0.0.1:5014

TO QUIT:
Close the Terminal window, or press Ctrl-C in it.

NEW IN v6.0 — RAW SUPPORT:
- RAW files are curated alongside JPEGs: Canon CR2/CR3, Nikon NEF,
  Sony ARW, Adobe DNG, Fuji RAF, Olympus ORF, Panasonic RW2 and more.
- Fast: the full-size preview your camera embeds in every RAW is used
  for thumbnails and analysis — EXIF (date, lens, GPS) included.
- Shooting RAW+JPG? In Cull, use the "All types / RAW only / JPG only"
  filter chips; RAW frames carry a purple RAW tag.
- Exports always copy your original RAW files, untouched.
- Running from source instead of this bundle? RAW needs one extra
  library: pip install rawpy
  (Without it the app still works, JPEG-only, and says so in the sidebar.)

NOTES:
- 100% offline and local. Nothing is uploaded.
- Uses port 5014 (50mm f/1.4); macOS reserves port 5000 for AirPlay.
- If the window shows a Python version that isn't 3.9, tell me and I'll
  rebuild the libraries to match.
